Makep-UP

    A comprehensive makeup list includes face makeup (primer, foundation, concealer, setting powder, blush, highlighter, contour, bronzer), eye makeup (eyeshadow, eyeliner, mascara, brow pencil/gel), lip products (lipstick, lip liner, lip gloss), and essential tools (makeup brushes, sponges, beauty blender). Setting spray and makeup remover are also important for completing the look and taking it off. Here's a more detailed breakdown:

    Face:

  1. Foundation: Evens out skin tone and provides coverage.
  2. Concealer: Hides blemishes and dark circles.
  3. Setting Powder: Locks in foundation and concealer, controls shine.
  4. Blush: Adds a pop of color to the cheeks.
  5. Highlighter: Adds a luminous glow to the high points of the face.
  6. Contour: Adds dimension and definition to the face.
  7. Bronzer: Adds warmth and a sun-kissed look.

    8. Eyes:

  8. Eyeshadow: Adds color and dimension to the eyelids.
  9. Eyeliner: Defines the eyes and creates different looks.
  10. Mascara: Lengthens, volumizes, and defines eyelashes.
  11. Brow Pencil/Gel: Fills in and shapes the eyebrows.

    12. Lips:

  12. Lipstick: Adds color and definition to the lips.
  13. Lip Liner: Helps define the lip shape and prevents feathering.
  14. Lip Gloss: Adds shine and dimension to the lips.

    15. Tools:

  15. Makeup Brushes: Used for applying and blending makeup.
  16. Sponges/Beauty Blenders: Used for blending foundation, concealer, and other products.
  17. Makeup Remover: Removes makeup effectively.
  18. Setting Spray: Helps set makeup and make it last longer.
