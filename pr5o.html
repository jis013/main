Understanding Circuit Boards

It is commonly known as printed circuit boards (PCBs), or circuit boards; they are essential components

in electronics nowadays. They form the basis for the electronic circuits that allow for the interconnection

of many electronic components, enabling their combined operation. In this manner, knowing the basic parts and structure

of a circuit board allows you to easily comprehend their significance as well as how they function.
